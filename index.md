# Wiki Index

## Concepts
- [[concepts/eudaimonia]] — Happiness as complete, self-sufficient being-at-work of the soul in accordance with virtue ( #philosophy #ethics #virtue-ethics)
- [[concepts/energeia]] — Being-at-work, Aristotle's central ontological term distinguishing complete activity from mere process ( #philosophy #ethics #metaphysics)
- [[concepts/hexis]] — Active condition/character, the genus of virtue and vice, acquired by habituation ( #philosophy #ethics #virtue-ethics)
- [[concepts/doctrine-of-the-mean]] — Virtue of character as a mean between vices of excess and deficiency, with the full virtue table ( #philosophy #ethics #virtue-ethics)
- [[concepts/akolasia]] — Dissipation, the vice of excess opposite temperance; literally "unpruned" in Greek ( #philosophy #ethics #virtue-ethics)
- [[concepts/to-kalon]] — The beautiful, the end for the sake of which virtuous action is chosen ( #philosophy #ethics #virtue-ethics)
- [[concepts/prohairesis]] — Choice and the analysis of voluntary/involuntary action underlying moral responsibility ( #philosophy #ethics #virtue-ethics)
- [[concepts/phronesis]] — Practical judgment and the intellectual virtues; mutual dependence of judgment and character ( #philosophy #ethics #epistemology)
- [[concepts/akrasia]] — Lack of self-restraint, distinct in kind from both vice and virtue ( #philosophy #ethics #moral-psychology)
- [[concepts/justice-nicomachean]] — Justice as complete virtue toward others, natural vs. conventional justice, and equity ( #philosophy #ethics #political-philosophy)
- [[concepts/distributive-justice]] — Particular justice's geometric-proportion species: shares of common goods ( #philosophy #ethics #political-philosophy)
- [[concepts/corrective-justice]] — Particular justice's arithmetic-proportion species: transactions, reciprocity, currency ( #philosophy #ethics #political-philosophy)
- [[concepts/philia]] — Friendship in three species (utility, pleasure, virtue); a friend as "another self" ( #philosophy #ethics #virtue-ethics)
- [[concepts/pleasure-aristotle]] — Pleasure as the unimpeded being-at-work of a natural capacity, not a process ( #philosophy #ethics #moral-psychology)
- [[concepts/contemplative-life]] — Theoretical contemplation as complete happiness ( #philosophy #ethics #metaphysics)
