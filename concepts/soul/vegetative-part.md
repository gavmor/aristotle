# Vegetative Part (Phutikon)

The vegetative or nutritive part of the soul is the strictly biological subdivision of the [[concepts/soul/irrational-part|irrational soul]] (Book I, Chapter 13).

## Key Ideas

- **Deaf to Reason**: It governs nutrition, growth, and sleep. Aristotle explicitly notes that this part has no share in reason whatsoever and cannot be persuaded by it.
- **Excluded from Ethics**: Because it functions automatically and does not participate in rational choice, it falls entirely outside the scope of human moral virtue.
