# Knowing Part (Epistēmonikon)

The knowing part (sometimes translated as the scientific part) is one of the two subdivisions of the [[concepts/soul/rational-part|rational soul]] (Book VI, Chapter 1). 

## Key Ideas

- **Invariable Objects**: It is dedicated strictly to contemplating things whose governing principles are [[synthesis/necessity-and-contingency|incapable of being otherwise]] (i.e., eternal, necessary truths). 
- **Truth-Disclosing Powers**: It houses the intellectual virtues of **[[concepts/nous|intellect]]** (grasping first principles), **knowledge** (*epistēmē* - demonstrating from principles), and **[[concepts/sophia|wisdom]]** (the perfection of both).
